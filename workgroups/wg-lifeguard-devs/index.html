Workgroup: Lifeguard Devs

People who maintain OSS in the Lifeguard programe.

ID: wg-lifeguard-devs
Discord: #wg-lifeguard-devs

The Lifeguard Devs workgroup handles emergency maintenance of open-source projects, that are in the Lifeguard project. The overall work on the Lifeguard project is done in wg-lifeguard instead.

Join when you are interested in maintaining open-source projects in need.
