About

Team Open directly supports maintainers of Open Source Software.

Current status: ideas

We’re just getting started with ideas. Team Open was called into being September 13th 2019 and we’re now looking for like minded-people to join us. If that’s you, contact us! The details are on the community page.

Why?

© 2014 CommitStrip.com

Many open source projects do not receive the broad support and contributions as the first picture of open source would have you believe.

While a few maintainers dilligently offer their skills, time and money to build software we’re all relying on, the rewards are often more work, anxiety and guilt.

What

This team is by and for the doers. We start projects that are achievable right now and may provide help. Any relief we can offer today is a win.

For the long term, we spread the message and hope to make clear, this is a serious problem we need to address at a societal level.

What about a discussion platform?

These exist, so while Team Open will likely have a good amount of people to have in depth discussions with, the primary goal is around moving into action.
